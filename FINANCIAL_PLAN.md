## The Runway — Self-Employment Plan

**Goal:** Graduate high school Oct 31 2027 → go self-employed (online/digital) full-time, debt-free, with enough banked to live ~12 months without a wage while building the business.

**The council verdict (run twice, once on Paidy, once on the full runway plan):**

> Commit to the save-first runway plan — **but quit the wage on evidence (real online income), not on graduation day.**

Build the online business **in parallel with the wage now.** The runway fund accumulates as a byproduct. Make the leap when income proves it can carry you — which may be before, on, or after graduation. Never fix "quit on Nov 1 2027" as a calendar commitment; decide in mid-2027 based on real income data.

**Why:** A runway is a bridge to income. ¥610k buys ~11 months of survival, but it buys nothing if there's no income on the far side. Earning ramp — not the savings number — is the variable that determines success. Saving is the seatbelt; the online business is the car.

**Biggest risk:** Spending the whole runway while the business still earns ~¥0. Defuse with:
1. Separate ¥50k emergency buffer before the runway proper
2. Never quit the wage until real income exists
